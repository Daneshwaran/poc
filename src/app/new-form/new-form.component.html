NDA Agreement Non-Disclosure Agreement for Nokia HR Applications:

The purpose of this document is to emphasize the confidentiality related to HR Access Rights. With the HR Access Rights you will gain accesses to certain Nokia HR Applications (defined in the HR Access Rights intranet page) and to personal data of Nokia employees and External Resource in your responsibility area in those Nokia HR Applications.

Therefore we require you to read carefully this non-disclosure agreement and accept it in order to receive HR Access Rights.

By signing this I agree to do the following:
  • Undertake to keep strictly confidential any and all personal data I have access to or that is made available to me in any form or manner. I will not discuss, disclose or otherwise make the personal data available to any unauthorized persons within Nokia or to anyone outside Nokia.
  • Not disclose personal data to or discuss the data with any unauthorized persons. This also means that I will not disclose to or discuss with any line manager about personal data concerning any employee outside of the organization the line manager is responsible for. In all cases I will only disclose or discuss personal data with authorized persons on a need to know basis.
  • Not use or review personal data for my own interest but only when it is needed to perform my work responsibilities. Unnecessary data mining can be monitored and regarded as failure to follow data protection guidelines.
  • Not maintain my own employment related data in Nokia HR applications such as P24 (SAP HR system) or Manager Self Services (MSS).
  • Not approve any myHRportal requests that relate to my own employment.
  • Maintain in myHRportal under Employee Self Services (ESS) only my own employment related data. The data entered in ESS must be accurate and legally correct.
  • Not deliver my username and password to any other user.
  • Respect and fully abide by all data protection and privacy laws and regulations and related Nokia internal policies and guidelines.
  • Respect and follow the confidentiality obligations by using the information only in an appropriate ways never disclosing details to unauthorized persons.
  • Always attempt to compile reports that do not contain any personal data. Reports should contain personal data only when this is necessary for a specific business purposes and even then must not contain unnecessary or excessive amounts of personal data.
  • Disclose reports containing personal data only to authorized persons having a clear need to know such information. I will deliver these reports only inside Nokia.
  • Delete the report once it is no longer necessary for the purpose it was originally provided to me for.

Violations of the obligations set out here or failure to handle personal data in a professional manner may subject to disciplinary action (including termination of employment), sanctions, damages or legal action by Nokia. This agreement is also binding after leaving Nokia.
